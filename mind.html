STILLNESS

Allow yourself to find a posture where your head, neck and torso are in alignment.Through this posture you'll allow your body to be at a still point where a sense of physical stillness sets in.This will not only help steady your body but also your mind.

RELAXED AND EFFORTLESS

Be aware of your flow of breathe.By breathing you can feel it flow through your abdomen and sides of your rib cage as you breath in and out.This becomes a calm and natural state, for a long period of time it will deepen and become more smooth.Through this state all thoughts become irrelevant and you'll be in a zen state.

MANTRA

Matra is a word often used to practise concentration like so'ham(pronounced "so-hum").Through this you allow your awareness to rest in sound of mantra and eventually it will arise spontaneously in your mind.

NOSTRIL BREATHING

By breathing through your nostrils a calming focus will take place and you feel grounded in your state of breathing in and out.By doing this you can feel a flow of energy of the body and mind which creates a sense of quietness and joy.

REDUCES PAIN

Whether its physical or emotional pain you feel,Meditation helps greatly reduces thoses flaws. through this method you'll feel at ease with yourself and greatly improves health and boost your mood.
